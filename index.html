Visit Newburgh

Explore the history and culture of this charming town on the Ohio River!

Top 3 activities to do in Newburgh

Walk the Rivertown Trail

Take this beautiful paved trail along the riverfront and through woods.

Explore Newburgh's dowtown area

Spend your afternoon seeing historic buildings, shopping at the local boutiques, and end with dinner on the riverfront.

Spend some time at the local community park

Lots of fun to be had with a play structure, splash pad, sports field and courts, and more!
